Title

Front cover of photo memory album "Kamrater från Kristinehams-Praktiska-skola 1917-1918" belonging to Signe Elvira Elgh (b. 1900 Molkom, Sweden)

Description

Front outside cover of photo memory album made of paperboard covered in dark blue embossed velvet with an engravable silvertone shield placed in the middle and a narrow silvertone plate on the fore-edge belonging to Signe Elvira Elgh b. 1900 entitled "Kamrater från Kristinehams-Praktiska-skola 1917-1918"
